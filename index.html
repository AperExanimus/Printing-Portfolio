Filament Purge Mitigation

Manually changing out filament mid-print will cause old filament to purge. If this happens over the model it will ruin the print. The long term fix will likely be a firmware upgrade. In the meantime I add these squares to the build in order to guide the nozzle to a safe area during filament changes.

QR Code Tests

Air Quality Considerations

While most makers I meet are not concerned with air quality during PLA prints, this is something that I intend to research further. I decided that building an enclosure and air purifier would both be good practice.

Ikea Lack Enclosure

This was my first attempt at an Ikea Lack enclosure. Initially I tried to apply weather stripping to the door to get a better seal. This is going to require a redesign of the printed couplers in order to hold the gasket flush though.

Second acrylic panel added with PETG door handles. I used a soldering iron to heat insert magnets for an easy locking mechanism.
